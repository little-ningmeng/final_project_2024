Hello and welcome to my page!

Here, you'll find a comprehensive collection of the restaurants I've had the pleasure of visiting this year in Ann Arbor.

This website is solely dedicated to documenting my food journey and is not intended for critique or evaluation purposes. Each restaurant has been categorized to provide you with a convenient browsing experience, which you can explore further on the restaurant page.

Explore the rich variety of culinary delights, from the flavors of East Asian cuisine including Chinese, Korean, and Japanese delicacies, to the vibrant spices of Mexican and Indian fare. Take your time to peruse the pages and unearth new dining destinations amidst the vibrant culinary landscape of Ann Arbor.

Thank you for visiting, and I hope you enjoy your time exploring the diverse array of dining experiences showcased here!
